## About Me
I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Mathematics at the University of Pennsylvania, interested in algebraic geometry and enumerative geometry. More specifically, I work on Donaldson–Thomas theory, Pandharipande–Thomas theory, wall-crossing, and related motivic and cohomological refinements.

I completed my B.S. and M.S. in Mathematics at National Taiwan University. My master's thesis, advised by [Hsueh-Yung Lin](https://homepage.ntu.edu.tw/~hsuehyunglin/), studied the motivic DT/PT correspondence for Calabi–Yau threefolds. I have also had the opportunity to study with [Adeel Khan](https://www.preschema.com) at the Institute of Mathematics, Academia Sinica, on topics related to derived algebraic geometry and enumerative geometry.
